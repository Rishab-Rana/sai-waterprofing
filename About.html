About Us


Typically, extensive waterproofing measures are applied during construction to ensure that moisture is controlled at the initial stage. Waterproofing can also be done after a building has been built to solve problems as they arise. Opting for the right waterproofing contractors is crucial for several reasons - it ensures health for your family who experiences blurred minds when in contact with water for extended periods, water damage in the event of an earthquake will be minimal because underground pipes tend not to crack due to consistent pressure on them, sinking foundations is prevented when the waterproofing is secure enough beneath buildings and lastly but most importantly when your house floods you can relax! Sai waterproofing experts know how to meet these standards of top-quality waterproofing!

Frequently Asked Questions


Our polyurethane coatings have two components, a resin and an activator, which are mixed together. When mixed, the two parts react together to form our coating just prior to application. This ensures maximum stability during storage as the reaction takes place just before application.

Our PIV Primer is a special damp-tolerant Polyurethane Primer which seals the concrete prior to application of the main coat. It allows for application even in high humidity environments such as coastal or rain-laden areas.

Drythane has an expected service life of over 25 years. On average, our coating can last over 3-4 times the expected life of competing products. We also provide an optional reflective aliphatic coating, applied on top of the main coat, which can provide insulation for over 25 years!
